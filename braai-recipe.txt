Ingredients:
 - 30 days mature rump steak
 - Salt
 - Braai Salt
 
Directions:
 1. Make a Barbeque fire with hot coals 
 
 2. Put the Steak on a grille over the hot coals
 
 3. Throw salt over any fat attached to steak

 4. Sprinkle Braai Salt over steak.

 5. Wait a minute

 6. Turn steak over

 7. Repeat step 3

 8. Repeat step 4

 9. Turn every 2 minutes for 6 minutes

 10. Check for medium by cutting a slice

 11. Depending on how the steak should be done, take the steak off the grille and serve immediately with Red wine
